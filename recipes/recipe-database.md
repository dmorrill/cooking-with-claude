## Lightning Fast (≤15 min)

### Garlic Butter Pasta
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Cook Time:** 10 min | **Servings:** 2

**Ingredients:**
| Item | Amount | Location |
|------|--------|----------|
| Pasta (any shape) | 8 oz | Pantry |
| Butter | 3 tbsp | Refrigerator |
| Garlic | 4 cloves | Refrigerator |
| Parmesan | 1/2 cup | Refrigerator |
| Red pepper flakes | 1/4 tsp | Pantry |
| Fresh parsley | 2 tbsp | Refrigerator |

**Instructions:**
1. Cook pasta according to package directions, reserve 1 cup pasta water
2. Melt butter in pan, add minced garlic, cook 1 minute
3. Toss pasta with butter, add pasta water as needed
4. Top with parmesan, red pepper flakes, and parsley
