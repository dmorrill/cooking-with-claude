# Spicy Mushroom Lasagna (Make-Ahead)
- **Prep Time**: 30 minutes
- **Cook Time**: 45-60 minutes
- **Total Time**: 75-90 minutes
- **Yield**: 8-10 servings
- **Difficulty**: Medium
- **Type**: Make-Ahead Main Dish
- **Source**: Holiday menu

### Ingredients

#### Mushroom Tomato Sauce
- [ ] **Olive oil** (2-3 tbsp) - *Location: pantry*
- [ ] **Onion or shallot** (1 large, diced) - *Location: refrigerator*
- [ ] **Garlic** (4-6 cloves, minced) - *Location: refrigerator*
- [ ] **Cremini mushrooms** (1 lb, sliced) - *Location: refrigerator*
- [ ] **Mixed mushrooms** (8 oz - shiitake, oyster, etc.) - *Location: refrigerator*
- [ ] **Chili flakes** (1-2 tsp, to taste) - *Location: pantry*
- [ ] **Crushed tomatoes** (28 oz can) - *Location: pantry*
- [ ] **Salt and black pepper** - *Location: pantry*
- [ ] **Fresh thyme or oregano** (optional) - *Location: refrigerator*

#### Layers
- [ ] **Lasagna noodles** (1 box, no-boil or regular) - *Location: pantry*
- [ ] **Ricotta or cottage cheese** (15 oz) - *Location: refrigerator*
- [ ] **Mozzarella** (2 cups shredded) - *Location: refrigerator*
- [ ] **Parmesan** (1 cup grated) - *Location: refrigerator*

### Equipment
- [ ] **9x13 baking dish**
- [ ] **Large skillet or pot**
- [ ] **Aluminum foil**

### Instructions

#### 1. Make Mushroom Sauce
1. Heat olive oil in large skillet
2. Sauté onion until soft, add garlic
3. Add all mushrooms, cook until liquid evaporates
4. Add chili flakes, crushed tomatoes
5. Simmer 15-20 minutes
6. Season with salt, pepper, herbs

#### 2. Assemble Lasagna
1. Spread thin layer of sauce in baking dish
2. Layer: noodles, ricotta, sauce, mozzarella
3. Repeat layers 3-4 times
4. Top with remaining sauce and cheeses

#### 3. Bake
1. Cover with foil
2. Bake at **375°F** for 45-60 minutes
3. Uncover last 10-15 minutes to brown top
4. Let rest 10-15 minutes before cutting

#### 4. Cool and Store
Cool completely before refrigerating

### Notes

**Make-Ahead:**
- Can be assembled ahead and refrigerated
- Reheat gently for dinner
- Can be frozen for longer storage

**Reheating:**
- Cover with foil
- Bake at **350°F** for 30-40 minutes until heated through
- Can reheat individual portions in microwave

**Spice Level:**
- 1 tsp chili flakes = mild heat
- 2 tsp = medium-spicy
- Adjust to preference

**Variations:**
- **Vegan:** Use plant-based cheese alternatives
- Add spinach or kale between layers
- Use different mushroom combinations

**Storage:**
- Refrigerated: 4-5 days
- Frozen: Up to 3 months

**Holiday Timeline:**
- Make ahead several days before
- Reheat and serve for dinner
