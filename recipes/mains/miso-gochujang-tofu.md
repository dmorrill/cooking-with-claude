# Miso-Gochujang Tofu Sheet Pan

Easy weeknight vegan meal with crispy tofu and roasted vegetables.

**Prep Time:** 10 min (+ 30 min marinating) | **Cook Time:** 25-30 min | **Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

### Marinade
| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed | 1 block (14-16oz) |
| Miso paste | 2 Tbsp |
| Gochujang | 1 Tbsp (adjust to taste) |
| Soy sauce | 2 Tbsp |
| Sesame oil | 1 Tbsp |
| Fish sauce (optional) | 1 tsp |
| Garlic, minced | 3 cloves |
| Fresh ginger, grated | 1 inch |
| Rice vinegar (optional) | 1 tsp |

### Vegetables
| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Green beans, trimmed | 1 lb |
| Onions, cut into wedges | 2 medium |
| Oil for roasting | 2 Tbsp |
| Salt and pepper | to taste |

## Instructions

1. **Prep tofu**: Press to remove water, cut into 1-inch cubes
2. **Make marinade**: Whisk miso, gochujang, soy sauce, sesame oil, fish sauce, garlic, and ginger
3. **Marinate**: Coat tofu and let sit 30+ minutes (longer is better)
4. **Prep vegetables**: Preheat oven to 400°F. Toss beans and onions with oil, salt, pepper
5. **Arrange**: Place tofu and vegetables on sheet pan with space between pieces
6. **Roast**: Bake 25-30 minutes until tofu is golden and veggies have charred edges

## Notes

- **Tofu texture**: Press well for crispier results (15+ minutes under weight)
- **Don't overcrowd**: Space on pan = proper browning
- **Marinate ahead**: Up to 24 hours for deeper flavor
- **Storage**: Leftovers keep 3-4 days refrigerated
- **Reheat**: 400°F oven 5-10 minutes to restore crispiness

## Spice Levels

- **Mild**: 1 tsp gochujang
- **Medium**: 1 Tbsp gochujang (as written)
- **Spicy**: 2+ Tbsp gochujang

## Serving Suggestions

- Over steamed rice or quinoa
- With kimchi or pickled vegetables
- Topped with sesame seeds and green onions
- Fresh lime squeeze, extra soy sauce, cilantro, garlic chili sauce
