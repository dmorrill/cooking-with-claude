# Roasted Butternut Squash

Simple roasted cubes with olive oil and spices - perfect side dish for any season.

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 25-30 min | **Servings:** 4-6

## Ingredients

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Butternut squash, peeled and cubed | 650g (~1.5 lbs) |
| Olive oil | 2 Tbsp |
| Salt | ½ tsp |
| Black pepper | ¼ tsp |
| Optional seasonings | see variations below |

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C)
2. Peel butternut squash, remove seeds, and cut into 1-inch cubes
3. Place cubed squash in large bowl
4. Drizzle with olive oil and toss to coat evenly
5. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and any optional seasonings
6. Spread squash in single layer on parchment-lined baking sheet (don't crowd)
7. Roast 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden brown and fork-tender
8. Serve immediately as a side dish

## Flavor Variations

**Savory:**
- Add garlic powder, paprika, or curry powder

**Sweet:**
- Add cinnamon and a drizzle of maple syrup

**Spicy:**
- Add cumin and chili powder

## Notes

- **Even cooking**: Cut cubes to uniform 1-inch size
- **Don't crowd**: Spread in single layer with space between pieces for best caramelization
- **Make-ahead**: Can roast up to 3 days ahead; reheat in 350°F oven for 10 minutes
- **Storage**: Refrigerate in airtight container up to 5 days

## Uses

- Great as side dish
- Toss into salads
- Mix into grain bowls
- Mash for soup
- Purée for pasta sauce
- Add to smoothies
