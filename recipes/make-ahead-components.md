## Batch Cooking Tips

### Sunday Meal Prep
- Make 2-3 condiments for the week
- Prepare granola batch (lasts 2-3 weeks)
- Start yogurt culture if needed

### Storage Containers
- Glass jars for pickles and condiments
- Airtight containers for granola
- Mason jars for yogurt

### Labeling
- Date when made
- Expected use-by date
- Contents

## Shopping List for Components

*This section can be populated when ready to make a batch*

### For Chimichurri
- Parsley
- Cilantro
- Garlic
- Olive oil
- Red wine vinegar
- Red pepper flakes
- Salt

### For Scallion Ginger
- Scallions (green onions)
- Fresh ginger
- Neutral oil
- Soy sauce or tamari
- Rice vinegar

### For Pickled Mushrooms
- Fresh mushrooms (cremini, shiitake, or mixed)
- Vinegar (white wine or apple cider)
- Water
- Sugar
- Salt
- Spices (peppercorns, bay leaf, etc.)

### For Cultured Cashew Cheese
- Raw cashews
- Probiotic capsules or starter culture
- Salt
- Water

### For Granola
- Rolled oats
- Nuts (almonds, walnuts, pecans)
- Seeds (pumpkin, sunflower)
- Maple syrup or honey
- Coconut oil
- Vanilla extract
- Salt
- Optional: dried fruit, coconut flakes

### For Homemade Non-Dairy Yogurt
- Non-dairy milk base (soy, cashew, oat)
- Yogurt starter culture or probiotic capsules
- Thickener (optional): tapioca starch or agar
