# Pumpkin Parker House Rolls (Small Batch)

Autumn twist on classic Parker House with pumpkin puree - tender, golden-orange rolls perfect for Thanksgiving.

**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Rise Time:** 2-2.5 hrs | **Bake Time:** 20-25 min | **Yield:** 4 rolls

*Source: Adapted from Parker House Rolls + Sweet Potato Pull-Apart Rolls technique*

## Ingredients

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| All-purpose flour | ¾ cup (90g) + 1-2 Tbsp for adjusting |
| Instant yeast | ¾ tsp (2g) |
| Granulated sugar | 2¼ tsp (10g) |
| Salt | ⅓ tsp (2g) |
| Baking soda | ⅛ tsp (pinch) |
| Pumpkin puree | 3-4 Tbsp (~50g) |
| Unsalted butter (room temp) | 2¼ tsp (11g) |
| Unsalted butter (melted, for brushing) | 1 Tbsp (14g) |
| Buttermilk (room temp) | 3 Tbsp (43g) |
| Large egg | 1 Tbsp beaten (~¼ egg) |

## Instructions

### Step 1: Mix Pumpkin Base (5 minutes)
1. Mash pumpkin puree until smooth (no lumps)
2. Add room-temperature butter and buttermilk
3. Mix until well combined

### Step 2: Make Dough (5 minutes)
4. Combine flour, yeast, sugar, salt, and baking soda in separate bowl
5. Add pumpkin mixture and beaten egg
6. Stir until shaggy dough forms

### Step 3: Knead (10 minutes by hand)
7. Turn dough onto lightly floured surface
8. Knead until smooth and elastic
9. **Altitude check**: If too wet, add 1-2 Tbsp more flour

### Step 4: First Rise (60-75 minutes at altitude)
10. Place dough in greased bowl and cover
11. Let rise in warm spot until puffy

### Step 5: Shape Rolls (10 minutes)
12. Roll dough into 4" x 6" rectangle on clean surface
13. Brush with melted butter
14. Cut lengthwise into two strips
15. Fold each strip so bottom edge extends ½" beyond top
16. Cut into 4 rolls total

### Step 6: Second Rise (30-45 minutes)
17. Arrange rolls in greased 6-inch pan
18. Brush with butter and cover
19. Let rise until puffy and touching

### Step 7: Bake (20-25 minutes at 365°F)
20. Bake until golden brown
21. Brush with butter immediately and cool 5 minutes

## Notes

- **Pumpkin puree**: Use canned pure pumpkin (NOT pumpkin pie filling) or fresh roasted
- **Moisture adjustment**: Pumpkin adds liquid - may need extra flour
- **Flavor profile**: Subtle, earthy - pairs well with savory and sweet
- **Beautiful color**: Golden-orange hue even before baking
- **Holiday appeal**: Perfect for Thanksgiving
- **Storage**: Room temp 2-3 days, freeze up to 2 months

## Serving Suggestions

- Honey butter or maple butter
- Cranberry sauce for holidays
- Alongside soups, stews, or roasted turkey
