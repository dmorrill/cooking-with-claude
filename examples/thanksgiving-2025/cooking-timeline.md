## Morning: 9:00 AM - 12:30 PM

### 9:00 AM - Cake Assembly & Rolls Start
**Oven:** Off

- [ ] **Remove from fridge:** Lasagne, icing, cake layers (bring to room temp)

## Afternoon: 1:00 PM - 4:30 PM

### 1:00 PM - Green Bean Prep
**Stovetop:** Medium-high heat

- [ ] **Caramelize Onions** (25 minutes)
  - Cook with garlic, turmeric, salt
  - Stir in stock, cream, parmesan, mustard
  - Add pre-blistered green beans
  - Keep warm on low heat

### 1:45 PM - Sweet Potato Prep
**Stovetop:** Steam & skillet

- [ ] **Steam Sweet Potatoes** (30-40 minutes)
  - Until tender when pierced
- [ ] **Make Miso-Ginger Sauce** (10 minutes)
  - Pound garlic and ginger
  - Mix with miso, vinegar, oils

### 2:30 PM - Lasagne Bake
**Oven:** Preheat to 200Â°C fan (390Â°F)

- [ ] **Bake Lasagne - Uncovered** (12 minutes at 220Â°C/425Â°F)
  - Turn dish halfway
- [ ] **Grill/Broil** (2 minutes)
  - Watch for crispy edges
- [ ] **Rest** (5 minutes)
  - Drizzle with cream and oil, sprinkle parsley

---

## Final Hour: 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

### 4:00 PM - Sweet Potato Finishing
**Stovetop:** High heat skillet

- [ ] **Sear Sweet Potatoes** (3 min per batch)
  - Halve and score in crisscross
  - Sear cut-side down until caramelized
- [ ] **Plate and Sauce** (5 minutes)
  - Arrange on platter, spoon sauce over
  - Garnish with black sesame seeds

### 4:15 PM - Slaw Assembly
**No heat needed**

- [ ] **Toss Slaw** (5 minutes)
  - Combine prepped vegetables with dressing
- [ ] **Plate with Pecans** (2 minutes)
  - Top with candied pecans

### 4:30 PM - Green Bean Casserole Finishing
**Stovetop:** Reheat if needed

- [ ] **Finish Green Beans** (5 minutes)
  - Transfer to serving bowl
  - Squeeze lemon over top
  - Scatter parmesan and crispy rosemary onions

### 4:45 PM - Martini Mixing
**Bar setup**

- [ ] **Mix Pineapple Sage Martinis** (10 minutes)
  - Per serving: 100ml infused gin, 50ml pineapple puree, 25ml lemon juice, 20ml clove syrup
  - Shake with ice 10-15 seconds
  - Strain into chilled glasses
  - Garnish with sage leaf

---

## 5:00 PM - Dinner Time!

### Serving Order
1. **Cocktails** - Pineapple Sage Martinis

3. **Fresh sides** - Apple Fennel Slaw
4. **Bread** - Pumpkin Rolls (warm or room temp)

## If Running Behind

- **Skip martinis** - Serve wine instead
- **Simplify cake** - Use store-bought meringues
- **Delegate** - Ask guests to help with plating

## Temperature Holding

| Dish | How Long | Method |
|------|----------|--------|
| Lasagne | 20-30 min | Cover with foil |
| Green beans | 30 min | Lowest stovetop heat |
| Sweet potatoes | 15 min | Serve room temp |
| Rolls | 1 hour | Room temp is fine |
| Slaw | 30 min | Actually improves! |
