# Thanksgiving 2025 - Complete Meal Planning Example

This example shows how Claude can help you plan and execute a complex multi-dish holiday meal. Starting with a list of 7 dishes, Claude created:

- A consolidated shopping list
- A 4-day prep schedule
- A detailed day-of cooking timeline
- Oven and stovetop scheduling

## How to Use This as a Template

1. **Define your menu** - Tell Claude what dishes you want to serve
2. **Specify constraints** - Number of guests, dinner time, any dietary restrictions
3. **Ask for the plan** - "Create a prep schedule and shopping list for these dishes"
4. **Iterate** - Ask Claude to adjust based on your schedule or preferences

## Example Prompt

```
I'm hosting Thanksgiving dinner for 8 people at 5pm. I want to make:
- [Your dishes here]

Please create:
1. A consolidated shopping list
2. A prep schedule for the days leading up to Thanksgiving
3. A detailed cooking timeline for the day-of

I have one oven and four stovetop burners. I'm at 5,280 feet altitude.
```

Claude will analyze your recipes, identify make-ahead opportunities, and create a realistic plan that accounts for oven conflicts and prep time.
