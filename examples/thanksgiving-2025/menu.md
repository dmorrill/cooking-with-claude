# Thanksgiving 2025 Menu

**Date:** Thursday, November 27, 2025
**Serves:** 6-8 guests
**Theme:** Ottolenghi-inspired Thanksgiving with homemade cocktails

---

## Cocktails

### Pineapple and Sage Martini
Roasted pineapple puree, sage-infused gin, clove syrup, and fresh lemon.
*Prep ahead: Clove syrup and pineapple puree can be made days ahead*

---

## Sides & Salads

### Sweet Potatoes with Miso-Ginger Sauce
Steamed sweet potatoes, caramelized and topped with umami miso-ginger sauce.
**Serves:** 4-6
*Make ahead: Steam potatoes the day before, sear and sauce day-of*

### Blistered Green Bean Casserole with Parmesan and Rosemary
Charred green beans, caramelized onions, cream, parmesan, and crispy rosemary.
**Serves:** 6-8
*Make ahead: Blister beans and caramelize onions the day before*

### Apple and Fennel Slaw with Candied Pecans
Crisp hispi cabbage, thinly sliced fennel, fresh mint, honey-mustard dressing.
**Serves:** 4-6
*Make ahead: Candied pecans and dressing can be made 1-2 days ahead*

---

## Main

### Spicy Mushroom Lasagne
Rich, umami mushroom ragù with dried porcini, fresh mushrooms, and layers of creamy pecorino-parmesan filling.
**Serves:** 6
*Make ahead: Assemble the day before, bake day-of*

---

## Bread

### Pumpkin Parker House Rolls
Soft, buttery rolls with pumpkin puree and buttermilk.
**Yield:** Scale to 12-16 for Thanksgiving
**Time:** 3-3.5 hours including rising
*Fresh day-of for best results*

---

## Dessert

### Lemon and Labneh Mascarpone Layer Cake
Triple-layer lemon sponge with labneh-mascarpone icing and lemon marmalade.
**Serves:** 6-8
*Make ahead: Bake layers 2 days ahead, assemble day-of*

## Scaling Guide

- **Cocktails:** Each batch makes ~10 servings
- **Sides:** Double for 12-16 guests
- **Lasagne:** Make 2-3 pans for larger groups
- **Rolls:** Scale recipe 3-4x for 12-16 rolls
- **Cake:** 6-inch serves 6-8; scale to 9-inch for larger groups

## Dietary Notes

- **Vegetarian-friendly:** All dishes are vegetarian
- **Can be made vegan:** Mushroom lasagne ragù (omit cream), apple fennel slaw
- **Gluten-free adaptations:** Most sides work with modifications
