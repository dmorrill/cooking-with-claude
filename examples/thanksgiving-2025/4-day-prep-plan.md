# 4-Day Thanksgiving Prep Plan

**Scenario:** Thanksgiving Thursday, November 27
**Goal:** Minimize cooking on Thursday - mostly assembly and fresh rolls
**Strategy:** Spread prep work across Monday-Wednesday

---

## Shopping List Overview

### Produce
- Pineapple (1 large)
- Green beans (800g)
- Onions (5-6 total)
- Carrots (5 large)
- Celery (4 large ribs)
- Mushrooms: chestnut (750g), oyster (500g)
- Hispi cabbage (1 small)
- Fennel (1 large bulb)
- Apples (1-2 pink lady)
- Lemons (10-12)
- Sweet potatoes (4 medium)
- Fresh ginger (1 knob)
- Tomatoes (6-7 plum/Roma)
- Red bell peppers (2 large)

### Fresh Herbs
- Rosemary (15g)
- Basil (5g)
- Mint (15g)
- Sage (20+ leaves)
- Parsley (1 cup + extra)

### Dairy & Eggs
- Unsalted butter (412g)
- Heavy cream (280ml)
- Mascarpone (222g)
- Cream cheese (149g)
- Labneh (73g)
- Whole milk (217ml)
- Buttermilk (43g)
- Eggs (5 large)

### Cheese
- Parmesan (140g)
- Pecorino Romano (60g)

---

## Monday (Day 1 - Light Day)

**Total active time:** ~4 hours (mostly hands-off simmering)

### Tasks
- [ ] **Receive groceries and organize**
  - Herbs: Wrap in damp paper towel
  - Mushrooms: Keep in paper bag in fridge

- [ ] **Make Vegetable Broth** (2 batches, 2 hours each)
  - Chop vegetables, simmer 1.5 hours
  - Blend smooth, stir in miso
  - Reserve 650ml in fridge for cooking
  - Freeze rest in portions

- [ ] **Optional: Make Lemon Marmalade** (2 hours, mostly hands-off)
  - Need only ⅓ cup for cake
  - Extra keeps 1 month refrigerated

- [ ] **Infuse Gin for Martinis** (5 min active)
  - Crush cardamom, add with sage leaves to gin
  - Let steep at room temperature

---

## Tuesday (Day 2 - Main Baking Day)

**Total active time:** ~4 hours

### Morning
- [ ] **Bake Lemon Cake Layers** (2 hours)
  - Brown butter (10 min)
  - Make batter, bake three 6" layers (18-22 min each)
  - Make lemon syrup, brush on warm cakes
  - Cool completely, wrap, store at room temp

### Afternoon
- [ ] **Make Mushroom Lasagne Ragù** (2.5 hours)
  - Roast fresh mushrooms (30 min at 230°C)
  - Rehydrate dried mushrooms in hot stock (30 min)
  - Make ragù base (45 min)
  - Cool and refrigerate - DO NOT assemble yet

- [ ] **Make Candied Pecans** (35 minutes)
  - Roast with honey and fennel seeds
  - Cool completely, store at room temp

### Evening (Optional)
- [ ] **Make Slaw Dressing** (5 min)
  - Whisk vinegar, honey, mustard, lemon, oil
  - Store in jar in fridge

---

## Wednesday (Day 3 - Assembly & Prep Day)

**Total active time:** ~3 hours

### Morning
- [ ] **Assemble Mushroom Lasagne** (30 min)
  - Make cheese-herb mixture
  - Layer: 5 layers sauce/cheese, 4 layers pasta
  - Cover with foil, refrigerate - DO NOT bake yet

- [ ] **Make Mascarpone Icing** (15 min)
  - Beat all ingredients in stand mixer
  - Store in fridge - DO NOT assemble cake yet

### Afternoon
- [ ] **Prep Green Bean Components** (45 min)
  - Blister green beans in batches (15 min)
  - Fry rosemary in oil (2 min)
  - Caramelize onions (25 min)
  - Store separately in fridge

- [ ] **Prep Sweet Potatoes** (45 min)
  - Steam sweet potatoes (30-40 min)
  - Make miso-ginger sauce (5 min)
  - Refrigerate separately

### Late Afternoon
- [ ] **Prep Slaw Components** (30 min)
  - Slice cabbage, fennel, apples thin
  - Chop mint
  - Store each separately with damp paper towels

- [ ] **Cocktail Prep** (30 min)
  - Strain infused gin
  - Make clove syrup
  - Prep pineapple puree

### Evening
- [ ] **Final Checks**
  - Review Thursday timeline
  - Set out serving platters and glasses
  - Check roll ingredients

---

## Thursday (Thanksgiving Day - Easy Day!)

**Total active time:** ~2-3 hours

### Morning (9:00 AM - 12:30 PM)
- [ ] **Assemble Cake** (30 min at 9:00 AM)
  - Layer with icing and marmalade
  - Decorate with meringues
  - Refrigerate

- [ ] **Start Pumpkin Rolls** (9:30 AM)
  - Make dough, knead (15 min active)
  - First rise (90 min - hands-off)
  - Shape rolls (10 min)
  - Second rise (45-60 min - hands-off)
  - Bake (20-25 min)
  - Done ~12:45 PM

### Afternoon (2:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
- [ ] **Bake Lasagne** (2:30 PM, 30 min total)
  - Covered 15 min at 200°C
  - Uncovered 12 min at 220°C
  - Grill 2 min, rest 5 min

- [ ] **Final Cooking Sprint** (4:00 PM, 1 hour)
  - Score and sear sweet potatoes (15 min)
  - Reheat green beans with toppings (10 min)
  - Toss slaw with dressing (5 min)
  - Mix martinis (10 min)

### 5:00 PM - Dinner Time!

---

## Work Distribution Summary

| Day | Active Time | Focus |
|-----|-------------|-------|
| Monday | 4-4.5 hrs | Broth (hands-off simmering) |
| Tuesday | 4 hrs | Cake layers + mushroom ragù |
| Wednesday | 3 hrs | Assembly and component prep |
| Thursday | 2-3 hrs | Rolls rising + final cooking |

**Pro tip:** Most hard work happens Tuesday/Wednesday, leaving Thursday for final touches and enjoying your guests!
