## Money-Saving Tips

1. **Buy in bulk**: Spices from bulk bins
2. **Ethnic markets**: Often cheaper than specialty stores
3. **Make your own**: Preserved lemons, crème fraîche, labneh
4. **Freeze extras**: Herbs, citrus zest, ginger
5. **Share with friends**: Split bulk purchases
6. **Grow your own**: Herbs, microgreens

## Substitutions

### Common Swaps
- Shallots → Small onion + garlic
- Leeks → Onions (milder flavor)
- Crème fraîche → Sour cream + heavy cream
- Mascarpone → Cream cheese + heavy cream
- Harissa → Chili paste + spices
- Fish sauce → Soy sauce + lime (vegetarian)
