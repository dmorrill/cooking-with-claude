# Recipe Template

Copy this template when adding new recipes to the database.

---

## Basic Recipe Template

```markdown
### Recipe Name
**Prep Time:** X min | **Cook Time:** X min | **Servings:** X

**Ingredients:**
| Item | Amount | Location |
|------|--------|----------|
| Ingredient 1 | 1 cup | Pantry |
| Ingredient 2 | 2 tbsp | Refrigerator |

**Instructions:**
1. First step
2. Second step
3. Third step

**Notes:**
- Substitution options
- Storage instructions
- Tips for success
```

---

## Detailed Recipe Template (for complex recipes)

```markdown
### Recipe Name
**Source:** [Original source or "Family recipe"]
**Prep Time:** X min | **Cook Time:** X min | **Total Time:** X min | **Servings:** X

**Tags:** vegetarian, quick, meal-prep friendly

**Ingredients:**
| Item | Amount | Location | Prep Notes |
|------|--------|----------|------------|
| Onion | 1 medium | Refrigerator | Diced |
| Garlic | 3 cloves | Refrigerator | Minced |

**Equipment Needed:**
- Large skillet
- Cutting board
- Chef's knife

**Instructions:**

**Prep:**
1. Prep step one
2. Prep step two

**Cook:**
1. Cook step one
2. Cook step two

**Finish:**
1. Final assembly
2. Garnish and serve

**Tips:**
- Pro tip for better results
- Common mistakes to avoid

**Variations:**
- Vegetarian version: substitute X for Y
- Spicier version: add Z

**Storage:**
- Refrigerator: 3-4 days
- Freezer: Up to 3 months
- Reheating: Instructions here

**Nutrition (per serving):**
- Calories: ~XXX
- Protein: Xg
```
